Team Profile

About HuiTapp

HuiTapp was founded in China by Lin Zhongzhuo as a non-profit R&D interest team. Members include senior professionals from major internet and gaming companies with strong engineering and growth experience.

Team Nature

We are driven by curiosity, built on engineering excellence, and delivered through product thinking. Our focus is practical innovation in mobile applications and interactive entertainment.

Each project is advanced with a production mindset: testable, experience-ready, and continuously optimizable.

Team Composition

Founder and Organizer: Lin Zhongzhuo

Engineering Members: Professionals from Alibaba, Tencent, Baidu, and related top-tier organizations with years of app and game development experience.

Operations and Growth Members: Professionals from NetEase, Changyou, and similar companies with strengths in growth strategy and commercialization execution.

Operating Principles

1. Problem-Led Product Framing

Start from real user scenarios and validate core value before scaling scope.

2. Engineering Feasibility First

Maintain robust architecture, maintainability, and long-term extensibility from day one.

3. Data-Driven Iteration

Use instrumentation, experimentation, and operational feedback loops for continuous improvement.
